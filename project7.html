Story Behind Connect and Heal:

Driven by a passion to improve primary care and empower patients, CNH Care uses technology and a team of specialists to offer seamless "connect and heal" services. Inspired by the founder's personal experience with a flawed healthcare system, they make family doctors central to your care, guiding you through any health challenge, no matter where you are.

Observe and Understand:

Before you start creating, Design Thinking encourages you to listen first. By observing, having conversations, and immersing yourself in the users' experiences, you'll gain valuable insights. These insights will help you understand and define the problem more clearly.

Problem Statement:

Creating an Electronic Medical Records (EMR) system aims to provide a comprehensive and efficient platform for in-house doctors to manage patient information, consultations, prescriptions, and appointments.

Outcomes and Impact:

Creating an Electronic Medical Records (EMR) system aims to provide a comprehensive and efficient platform for in-house doctors to manage patient information, consultations, prescriptions, and appointments.

  • Reduced the tool's interaction time for doctors by 35-45% which directly increased the consultation time with patient.
  • Over the past 3 months, we completed 1,100+ consultations, a 27% increase compared to the previous 3 months.
  • Mapping data for diagnosis, symptoms, and medicines in the EMR provided insights that helped improve product features like templates and medicine requirements.

Understanding Market Needs for a Standout Product:

Before we even break ground, we'll gain in-depth insights into our product's potential. By researching its target audience and market fit, we'll ensure a laser-sharp focus on its value proposition. Benchmarking competitors and identifying gaps will pave the way for a unique offering that surpasses anything currently available.

In-Depth Business Analysis

We Conducted a stake holder Interview to understand and elaborate the problem statement so that we can understand long terms and short term goals for the product they are trying to build, Understanding long term goal will help us design the product in a way where we would also think about scalability factor of the product.

Business Objective

  • Access Medical consultation with doctors by providing minimal information for Corporte employee identification in the EMR Portal
  • Keep track of all medical records of patients to offer improved medical guidance
  • From Business side, migrate customer data from third party application to our platform in order to access efficient data accessibility and effective cross platform
  • Personalized experience tailored to each patient's medical record
  • Comprehensive Platform for Inventory Management, Efficient Doctor Allocation, and Medical Waste Handling

Preparing the Path for User-Driven Designs:

After soaking up all we can in the Empathize stage, the Define stage is where we connect the dots. We take all the user insights and research, crunch them together, and distill the essence into a clear, human-centered problem statement. This sets the stage for the journey ahead!

User Persona:

Walk a mile in their digital shoes: Personas are crafted companions, embodying user types within your target audience. With their unique needs and goals, they help us shed our own biases and embrace diverse perspectives. This empathy fuels better design decisions, paving the way for a seamless and satisfying user experience.

From Insight to Implementation:

Aligning business vision, user needs, and technical capabilities ensures a clear, engaging, and obstacle-free strategy. This focused approach helps teams prioritize resources and solve the right problems for their target users, leading to a successful and satisfying experience for all.

User Flow:

We are creating a user flow that replicates doctors' traditional pen-and-paper workflow, ensuring a seamless transition and a sense of familiarity.

Usability Testing:

Initial designs underwent usability testing and we got lot of inputs to fully meet user needs & business goals. Usability testing helps ensure if the design is heading towards its goal or not.

Insights from Usability

Integrating Insights into User Flow:

For medical information, simply hearing a doctor's voice wasn't enough. We realized patients crave the additional trust and clarity that comes from seeing their doctor's face alongside the explanation. It's a more personal and impactful way to convey medical information.
