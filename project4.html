Health Risk Assessment

  •   Project Name

    Health Risk Assessment

  •   Contribution:

    Individual

  •   Timeline:

    April - May 2023

  •   Deliverable:

    Mobile and Website

  •   Client Name:

    Confedential

Problem:

Gain insights into current health status, comprehend the risks of potential diseases/conditions, foster and sustain motivation for a healthy lifestyle, and strive to achieve health goals.

Values for Company:

  • Engage users to spend more time on the C&H platform
  • Contribute in C&H being the healthcare ecosystem for any individual where any healthcare services can be availed including understanding more about your current health
  • CMP (Condition management plans)
    • Get users to enroll in the specific CMP based on the given for the results from specific assessments.
    • Insights from HRA will be used to determine whether a user is eligible for the program and also to determine which condition program is applicable, this is done through AHC and consultation service taken by user post assessment, if at risk.

Values for Users:

  • Spread awareness about health risk among patients
  • To be healthy or maintain health goals, track health progress

Research

India has about 64.5 million diabetic patients. It has been projected that the number of diabetics is likely to increase to 87 million by 2030.

As per the International Diabetes Federation (IDF), more than half (66%) of Indians are unaware of their diabetic status.

Competitive Analysis

Comprehensive health risk assessment covering a wide range of topics, personalized recommendations, actionable insights, integration with other features & services. Many healthcare products, such as Eka Care, Fitterfly, Practo, and 1mg, offer this feature.

  • Eka Care has a strong focus on chronic disease management.
  • Fitterfly has a strong focus on weight loss and fitness.
  • Practo has a strong focus on online doctor consultations.
  • Medibuddy & Mfine has a strong focus on health symptoms.

User Flow:

I went through an iterative process to develop a user flow, continuously refining and making adjustments along the way. This involved creating multiple versions and making numerous improvements until reaching the ultimate, finalized iteration of the user flow.

Design Solution

Effect on the Solution:

CMP Usage: WNS: Of the 5,000 users who submitted health risk assessments (HRAs), 3,294 were at risk. Of the at-risk users,55% completed the Annual Health Checkup (AHC).
