Brief about Connect and heal :

Determined to transform primary care and empower patients, CNH care leverages technology and a network of specialists to deliver seamless "connect and heal" services. Fueled by a founder's personal experience with a broken healthcare system, they bring family doctors to the forefront, guiding you through any health challenge,anywhere you are.

Understand:

Before building, Design Thinking asks you to listen. Through observation, conversation, and immersion, you'll step into users' shoes and gather insights that continuously shape the problem definition you're trying to solve.

Problem Staement:

Current lab reports often lack engaging and interactive elements, hindering patient’s understanding and reducing the impact of scientific findings. A digital format presents an opportunity to create dynamic and immersive reports that effectively communicate complex data and results.

  • Independent user research: We became detectives, uncovering user needs through our own research efforts. This valuable intel fueled our design decisions.

Understanding Target Users:

Through stakeholder feedback, we established three primary user categories for our smart report. Recognizing the portal's potential reach due to the widespread demand for medical testing, we additionally subdivided each category to identify the specific user groups most likely to engage with the portal's functionality.

User Interview and Surveys:

Following initial research that identified three crucial user segments, we conducted in-depth interviews with individuals from diverse backgrounds across four cities. Our participants ranged in age from 16 to 70 and provided valuable insights from various perspectives. Here's a summary of the key findings.

Online Surveys:

Online survey is a set of questions, sent to a targeted group of users, that probes their attitudes and preferences. Surveys can be a quick, easy, and inexpensive way to obtain data questions you ask.

Perceptual Mapping:

User research exposed competitors – some loud, some hidden. A qualitative perceptual map (Experience vs. Features) plots them all, revealing gaps and guiding our strategic positioning.

Competitor Analysis:

By analyzing our competitors, we uncover unmet needs and under-explored functionalities within our target market. This insight allows us to develop unique features with high potential for user adoption.

Benchmarking:

Benchmarking empowers us to stay ahead of the curve. By measuring ourselves against industry leaders,we identify best practices, optimize processes, and refine strategies to achieve our business goals.

Gaps:

Gap analysis pinpoints areas where our product can be strengthened. By comparing our online performance to benchmark targets and competitor offerings, we identify opportunities for improvement and differentiation, paving the way for market leadership.

Failed Attempt

Our first attempt didn't quite hit the mark. The existing process mirrored competitor approaches, and "smart report" fell short of capturing its true capabilities. Time for a rethink!

Problems

We stood at that crossroads, hearts heavy with the realization that our UI overhaul, while aesthetically pleasing, hadn't brought us closer to our true goal: user-centered design. It was a tough pill to swallow, but we knew staying put wasn't an option.

  • The design was not interactive
  • The patient will still feel the need to talk to a doctor.
  • We had added some generic videos explaining about each profile but it was not personalized.

Tea Break Idea !!

Forget fancy brainstorming sessions! Sometimes, the best ideas strike in the most unexpected places. Take our case: two colleagues, fueled by tea and biscuits, overheard the Paytm sound box. And boom! Just like that, the light bulb lit up - a quirky solution to personalize generic content was born. "You have received Rs.42 in your account"

Paytm stores each digit of a number separately, opening up unexpected possibilities for combining them into different formats. For example, "42," "24," or even "420" can all be formed from just "4" and "2." This flexibility extends to larger numbers, like "1000," which can be manipulated to become "one thousand four hundred."

Limitation & Implementation:

For medical information, simply hearing a doctor's voice wasn't enough. We realized patients crave the additional trust and clarity that comes from seeing their doctor's face alongside the explanation. It's a more personal and impactful way to convey medical information.

Content & Video editing team support:

Collaborating with content and video experts, we tackled the challenge head-on. Our first step: meticulously mapping out all potential scenarios within each profile. Using an Excel sheet, we charted combinations like high triglycerides with normal HDL and LDL, or vice versa, ensuring every possibility was accounted for in our personalized video explanations.
